Sales Tax Codes

 

The Sales Tax Code window in the Sales workspace is available only if the US financial localization is enabled.

Sales tax rate are set up for each individual territory and then assigned to customers. Enter a new tax code by entering its description, a combined tax rate (state + county + city), in % (for example 9.25 for 9.25%), and optionally the collecting tax agency.

Then for customers you are required to collect sales tax indicate a corresponding sales tax code on the Financial tab in the Company window. The system then will automatically calculate sales tax in sales documents for Items indicated as taxable and create corresponding accounting transactions.
