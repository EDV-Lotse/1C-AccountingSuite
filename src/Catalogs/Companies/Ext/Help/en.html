Companies List

 

The Companies List stores information about companies you do business with - your prospects, customers and vendors. You can create new Companies as well as view, and save the list in electronic form for sending in e-mail.

The system uses a unified list where you store both customers and vendors as opposed to two separate lists, which allows you to easily address the situation when a company is both a customer and a vendor. You can also create two separate records if a company is a customer and a vendor, if it better suits your situation. Companies are tagged as customers or vendors to expedite data entry - the sales documents in the Customer field show only Companies that are tagged as Customers, the purchasing documents in the Vendor field show only Companies that are tagged as Vendors.

 

To enter a Company:

 

1. Open the Company window by choosing Create / Company in the Lists workspace. Alternatively you can create a new Company by selecting Create in the Companies list that is accessible from the Lists workspace.

2. Enter the Company ID and Name. The Name field is displayed in all print forms.

3. Indicate if the Company is a customer or a vendor or both.

4. On the Contact tab enter company's contact information.

5. On the Address tab enter company's address information.

6. On the Financial tab change the default currency if necessary.

For the US financial localization functionality enter if the vendor is 1099 eligible, the Company's tax ID, and its sales tax code.

Change the default expense and income accounts. Note that the income and expense accounts selected for the company are used for posting only if items in the documents are not selected from the Items lists, and instead only their descriptions are manually entered.

7. Save the Company.
