Settings

 

The Settings window is located in the Lists workspace and has several tabs with various system settings described below:

 

General Settings tab

 

  • Units of measure: turns on the units of measure (U/M) functionality  - the Units of Measure list (Lists workspace), the Units of Measure tab on the Items, and U/M and Quantity U/M fields in document line items.
  • Multi-location: turns on the multi-location functionality - the Locations list (Warehouse workspace), location fields in the documents, the Location Balances journal.
  • Multi-currency: turns on the multi-currency functionality - the Currencies catalog (Lists workspace), currency fields in the documents, the Exchange Rates list (Lists workspace).
  • Unbalanced GL Entry posting: allows you to post an unbalanced GL Entry - this is used for entering beginning balances and needs to be turned off otherwise.
  • Beginning balances date: they system will create beginning balances on this date.
  • Default payment terms: select a default payment term to be used in sales documents.
  • Default currency: your reporting currency.
  • Default location: your default warehouse location.
  • Advanced pricing: this functionality is described here.
  • Vendor name: enter your preferred name for your vendors, for example "Supplier" (default name - "Vendor").
  • Customer name: enter your preferred name your customers, for example "Patient" (default name - "Customer").

 

Financial Localization tab:

 

Turn on the financial localization you intend to use.

  • For the US financial localization the system enables the Sales Tax Codes, 1099 Categories lists, the taxation field in document line items, the 1099 Category field in the Chart Of Accounts, 1099 reports.
  • For the Brazil financial localization the system enables the CFOP, ICMS product groups, ICMS tax table lists, ICMS product group category on Items, and the corresponding fields for ICMS tax calculation in the Sales Invoice line items.
  • For the VAT financial localization the system enables VAT Codes, and VAT Return Form Boxes lists, the VAT Invoice document, and VAT fields in document line items and headers.

 

VAT tab:

 

Select default purchase and sales VAT codes that will be prefilled when a new item is created.

 

Posting Accounts tab:

 

Here you can change default accounts the system uses to automatically create transactions when you post documents. Note that the Income, COGS, Expense, and Inventory accounts can be overridden by accounts set up on Item and Company forms. Detailed information on how the accounts are used to create transactions is provided in the General Journal help section.
