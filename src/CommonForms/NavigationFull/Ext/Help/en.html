AccountingSuite

AccountingSuite is a cloud-based accounting, inventory, and order management software solution designed for small businesses to succeed.

In the world of small business, Cash is King. The simple to use browser-based interface of AccountingSuite allows for flexible Invoicing, Cash Sales and Cash receipts. All from your iPad, MacBook, PC, laptop and mobile device.

Close deals more efficiently and effectively with cloud-based AccountingSuite. Enter Quotes and email to your prospect, create Sales Orders and ship product that same day, check inventory on the fly to make better business decision with customers, and improve "Order to Cash" timeframes. Inventory-oriented businesses will benefit from the solution's multi-warehouse support, easy to use procurement system, flexible purchase order document flow, and advanced inventory costing procedures not found on many other cloud-based competitors. And all this, from any computer or device with an internet connection.

AccountingSuite helps your business save money by eliminating the inefficiencies and providing you with real-time information during your day-to-day processes and decision-making. In addition, the software supports US GAAP requirements and IFRS standards for companies with operations outside the US.

To perform initial setup

  1. Specify Accounting settings.

    To be able to enter beginning balances in step 4, select the Unbalanced G/J entry posting and Beg. balances in documents check boxes.
  2. Enter Companies and Items.
  3. Enter Chart of accounts.
  4. Enter the beginning balances using the following documents:
    • General journal entries
    • Purchase invoices and Sales invoices
    • Purchase returns and Sales returns
    • Inventory beginning balances
  5. Check the balances by running various balance reports in the Accounting section. Make corrections to the entered documents if needed.
  6. To prohibit further editing of beginning balances, in Accounting settings, clear the Unbalanced G/J entry posting and Beg. balances in documents check boxes.


The application desktop contains the following areas:

  • Navigation area. Provides links to application sections and allows quick creation of accounting documents.
  • Full text search. Provides full text search in documents.
  • Quick reports. Allows quick report generation.
