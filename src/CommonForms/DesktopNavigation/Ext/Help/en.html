Solution Overview

 

Congratulations on purchasing 1C:Accounting Suite!

 

1C:Accounting Suite is a small business accounting and inventory software. The solution supports US GAAP and IFRS accounting and reporting standards.

 

The software provides full visibility into purchasing and sales transactions, automates accounts receivable and accounts payable. 1C:Accounting Suite makes managing payments, receipts, and bank transactions a breeze, providing business owners with a real-time financial snapshot of operations.

 

Inventory-oriented businesses will benefit from the solution's multi-warehouse support, flexible document flow, and advanced inventory costing procedures.

 

Start using the system by navigating to the necessary sections using the main menu or the navigation map on the system desktop.

 

You can find detailed information about the system in the following help sections:

 

  • Sales
  • Purchasing
  • Warehouse
  • Banking
  • Accounting
  • Lists

 

Note, that by default some functionality is turned off. You can turn on the units of measure, multi-location, and multi-currency functionality in Lists / Settings.
