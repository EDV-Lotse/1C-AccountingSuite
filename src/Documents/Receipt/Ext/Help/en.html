Receipts

 

The Receipt is a document used to record Sales Invoice and Purchase Return incoming payments. You can create new Receipts as well as view, print, and save them in electronic form for sending in e-mail.

 

To enter a Receipt:

 

1. Open the Receipt window by choosing Create / Receipt in the Banking workspace. Alternatively you can create a new Receipt by selecting Create in the Receipts list in the Banking workspace.

2. Change the default date, if necessary. The date is used for General Journal posting.

3. Select the Company that is paying you.

4. Indicate if the payment is deposited directly - "Account", or is deposited later individually or grouped with other payments - "Undeposited funds".

Select the account that will receive the funds (the field is available for editing only if the Account option is selected in the Deposit Type box).

Select the Payment Method.

5. In line items the system will display all unpaid documents for the Company that is paying you with amounts due.

6. Enter the amounts that are being paid against each unpaid document.

7. Save the Receipt. The system will update Accounts Receivable and Accounts Payable (for Purchase Returns) and generate General Journal postings. Detailed information about generated posting transactions is provided in the General Journal section.
