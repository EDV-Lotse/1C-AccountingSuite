Transfer Orders

 

Transfer Order is a document that records transfers between warehouses within your company and makes corresponding changes in the Location Balances journal. You can create new Transfer Orders as well as view, print, and save them in electronic form for sending in e-mail.

 

To enter a Transfer Order:

 

1. Open the Transfer Order window by choosing Create / Transfer Order in the Warehouse workspace. Alternatively you can create a new Transfer Order by selecting Create in the Transfer Orders list in the Warehouse workspace.

2. Change the default date, if necessary.

3. Select warehouse locations From and To.

4. Enter line items of the Transfer Order by selecting Items.

The system will automatically fill in the Cost, Line Total, and Document Total values. The cost value (and the calculated totals) are based on the last item cost and don't reflect the actual cost of inventory layers in the Inventory Journal. The system tracks actual costs in the Inventory Journal, and the amounts in the Transfer Order are shown for information only.

5. Save the Transfer Order. The system will reflect the transfer in the Location Balances journal.
