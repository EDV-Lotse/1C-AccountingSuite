Bank Reconciliations

 

The Bank Reconciliation is a tool used to simplify a monthly bank closing process. The document compares receipts and payments recorded in the system with the bank statement. You can create new Bank Reconciliations as well as view, print, and save them in electronic form for sending in e-mail.

 

 

To enter a Bank Reconciliation:

 

1. Open the Bank Reconciliation window by choosing Create / Bank Reconciliation in the Banking workspace. Alternatively you can create a new Bank Reconciliation by selecting Create in the Bank Reconciliations list in the Banking workspace.

2. Change the default date, if necessary. The date is used for General Journal posting of Service Charge and Interest Earned amounts.

3. Enter Statement From Date and Statement To Date from your bank statement. The system won't allow you to enter a period that overlaps with another Bank Reconciliation.

4. After you enter the Statement To Date the system will fill in line items of the Bank Reconciliation with system documents that are reflected in the bank statement - Deposits, Payments, and Cash Purchases in the selected time period.

5. Enter Beginning Balance, Ending Balance, Service Charge, and Interest Earned amounts from your bank statement.

6. Comparing the amounts listed by the system with your bank statement clear the amounts by checking the Cleared check box against the cleared amount. During the reconciliation process the system updates the Cleared Amount, Cleared Balance, and Difference fields in real time. In case of a successful reconciliation the Difference will be zero. Locate and fix discrepancies, if necessary.

7. Save the Bank Reconciliation. The system will generate General Journal postings for the Service Charge and Interest Earned amounts. Detailed information about generated posting transactions is provided in the General Journal section.
