The warehouse transfer is a document that records inventory transfers between warehouses within your company and makes corresponding changes in the Inventory journal.

To enter a warehouse transfer

  1. In the Warehouse section, click Warehouse transfers, and then click Create.
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • Number. By default the system assigns consecutive numbers when documents are saved, and no user input is required.

      You have the option to enter a custom number instead. Document numbers can include prefixes. For example, if you specify the number "T-001", your next deposits are automatically numbered "T-002", "T-003", and so on.
    • Date. The transfer date.

      This field is prefilled with the current date.
    • Location from. The "from" warehouse.
    • Location to. The "to" warehouse.
  3. In the Items list, add the items and quantities.

    The system automatically fills in the Cost, Line total, and Total values. The cost value (and the calculated totals) are based on the last item cost and do not reflect the actual cost of inventory layers in the Inventory journal. The system tracks actual costs in the inventory journal, and the amounts in the warehouse transfer document are shown for information only.
  4. In the Memo field, you can enter notes on the warehouse transfer.
  5. Click Post and close.
