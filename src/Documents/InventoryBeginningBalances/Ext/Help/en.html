The inventory beginning balance document records a beginning inventory balance.

Inventory beginning balances generate inventory journal transactions. For detailed information about transactions that are generated when inventory beginning balances are posted, see the Inventory journal help section.

To enter a beginning balance

  1. In the Accounting section, click Inventory beginning balances and then click Create.
  2. Enter or select the following:
    • Item. The item name.
    • Location. The location of the warehouse where the items are stored.

      This field is only available if the Multilocation check box is selected in Accounting settings. 
    • Date. The date of the inventory journal transaction that represents the beginning balance.

      This field is prefilled with the current date.
    • Quantity. The item quantity.
    • Value. The total item value.
  3. Click Post and close.
