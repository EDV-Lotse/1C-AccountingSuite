Chart of accounts

 

The system includes a basic chart of accounts you can customize for your needs. Every account is assigned one of the account types described below:

 

  • Accounts Payable. Typical balance - credit.
  • Accounts Receivable. Typical balance - debit.
  • Accumulated Depreciation. Typical balance - debit.
  • Bank. Typical balance - debit.
  • Cost Of Sales. Typical balance - debit.
  • Equity. Typical balance - credit.
  • Expense. Typical balance - debit.
  • Fixed Asset. Typical balance - debit.
  • Income. Typical balance - credit.
  • Inventory. Typical balance - debit.
  • Long Term Liability. Typical balance - debit.
  • Other Current Asset. Typical balance - debit.
  • Other Current Liability. Typical balance - credit.
  • Other Expense. Typical balance - debit.
  • Other Income. Typical balance - credit.
  • Other Non-Current Asset. Typical balance - debit.

    •  

    For the US financial localization assign 1099 Categories to accounts. Several accounts can have the same 1099 Category assigned to them.
