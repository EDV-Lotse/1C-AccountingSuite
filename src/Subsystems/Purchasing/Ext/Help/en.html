Overview

 

The Purchasing workspace in conjunction with the other system workspaces supports automating purchasing business processes - tracking purchase quotes, purchase orders, goods receipts, purchase invoices, payments, and purchase returns. Automatic document posting updates inventory balances and creates general journal transactions. You can generate individual document print forms, PDF/Excel versions of documents for sending via e-mail, as well as purchasing reports, such as A/P Aging.

The full implemented purchase transactions workflow is Purchase Quote -> Purchase Order -> Goods Receipt -> Purchase Invoice -> Payment.

The system also includes a standalone document - Cash Purchase that incorporates the goods receipt, purchase invoice, and payment purchasing steps into one transaction. The Purchase Return document is used to track returns.

By default the Purchase Quote, Purchase Order, Purchase Invoice, Cash Purchase, and Purchase Return documents are located in the Purchasing workspace, whereas the Goods Receipt documents in the Warehouse workspace, and the Payment documents in the Banking workspace. This default configuration can be changed to accommodate user requirements.

The system supports both real-time posting (updating inventory and creating general journal transactions), as well as batch posting. All purchasing documents except the Purchase Quote and Purchase Order generate posting. The Weighted Average, FIFO and LIFO costing methods are used for inventory costing. Detailed information about accounting transactions is provided in the General Journal section.
