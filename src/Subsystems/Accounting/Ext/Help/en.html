Overview

 

The system includes a completely set up accounting system out of the box - transactions are created in the General Journal when purchase or sales documents are posted, and reports, such as an income statement and a balance sheet are generated based on the General Journal data. You can also create individual General Journal Entries that can be used, for instance, for month closing or expense distribution.

Accounts that are used for automatic transaction posting are set up in Settings, and are overridden by accounts set up on Item, and Company (when Item ID is not filled in documents and instead item description is typed in) forms.

The accounting engine is IFRS and US GAAP compliant.

Detailed description of document posting rules is provided in the General Journal help section.
