The Accounting section automates your company's accounting processes. When purchase or sales documents are posted, transactions are created in the inventory journal (if inventory is involved) and general journal. In this section you can view a variety of accounting reports that are generated based on the general journal data.

The Accounting section contains the following documents:

  • Inventory beginning balance. Records beginning inventory balances.
  • General journal entry. Creates or voids accounting transactions.
     

The Accounting section contains the following journal:

  • General journal. Records all G/L account transactions.

The Accounting section contains the following lists:

  • Chart of accounts. Your company's chart of accounts.
  • 1099 categories. Stores 1099 vendor categories.

    This field is only available if the Sales tax (US) check box is selected in Accounting settings.
     
  • VAT codes. Stores value added taxes.

    This field is only available if the VAT check box is selected in Accounting settings.
     
  • Payment methods. Stores a list of payment methods (such as cash or credit cards). 
     

The Accounting section contains the following reports:

  • Income statement. Shows the income statement.
  • Income statement (detailed). Shows the detailed income statement.
  • Balance sheet. Shows the balance sheet.
  • Trial balance. Shows the trial balance.
  • Cash flow (direct). Shows the direct cash flow statement.
  • General ledger. Shows the general ledger.
  • Other comprehensive income. Shows the statement of other comprehensive income.
