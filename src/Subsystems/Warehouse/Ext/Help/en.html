Overview

 

The Warehouse workspace in conjunction with the other system workspaces supports automating warehouse business processes - receiving goods, issuing goods, and transferring goods between warehouses. You can generate individual document print forms, PDF/Excel versions of documents for sending via e-mail, as well as customer warehouse reports, such as inventory balances.

The Goods Receipt and Goods Issue documents are positioned in the middle of the Order -> Receiving/Issuing -> Invoicing workflow and can be used or not used depending on your situation. If the receiving or issuing wasn't performed by the warehouse documents it'll be performed by the invoices. The Goods Receipt and Goods Issue documents recognize your liability and assets respectively at the time of posting and automatically update inventory balances and create general journal transactions. The Sales Order and Purchase Order track the received/issued and invoiced amounts. Even if receiving/issuing is performed using the invoices, the received/issued column in the Purchase or Sales Order will still be updated.
