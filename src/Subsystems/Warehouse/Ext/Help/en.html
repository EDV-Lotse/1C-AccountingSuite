The Warehouse section automates warehouse business processes. Receiving or issuing goods is recorded by invoice documents. Automatic document posting updates inventory balances and creates general journal transactions.

The Warehouse section contains the following document:

  • Warehouse transfer. Records transfers between warehouses within your company and posts them in the inventory journal.
     

The Warehouse section contains the following journal:

  • Inventory journal. Records all inventory transactions.
     

The Warehouse section contains the following lists:

  • Item last costs. Stores last costs of purchased items.
  • Locations. Warehouse location list.

    This section is only available if the Multilocation check box is selected in Accounting settings.
      

The Purchasing section contains the following report:

  • Inventory balances. Shows inventory balances.
