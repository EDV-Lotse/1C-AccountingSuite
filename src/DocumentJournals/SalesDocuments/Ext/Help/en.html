Sales Documents Journal

 

The Sales Documents is an aggregate journal of all transactions available in the Sales workspace - Sales Quotes, Sales Orders, Sales Invoices, Cash Sales, and Sales Returns. The functionality of the Sales Documents is the same as of the individual document windows - you can create new sales documents as well as view, print, and save them in electronic form for sending in e-mail. You might find it useful to use this journal for your document flow and volume scenario.
