Purchasing Documents Journal

 

The Purchasing Documents is an aggregate journal of all transactions available in the Purchasing workspace - Purchase Quotes, Purchase Orders, Purchase Invoices, Cash Purchases and Purchase Returns. The functionality of the Purchasing Documents is the same as of the individual documents - you can create new purchasing documents as well as view, print, and save them in electronic form for sending in e-mail. You might find it useful to use this journal for your document flow and volume scenario.
